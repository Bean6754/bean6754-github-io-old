Lockscreen

Linux is a kernel which forms a major component of an operating system.

I will be talking about GNU/Linux whenever I refer to just 'Linux'.

The GNU part in GNU/Linux forms the coreutils (core utilities), inetutils (network utilities, not required), findutils (find utilities), etc..

Other Linux-based operating systems, like Google's Android have similar utilties. I.E: Android uses the Busybox utils, like Alpine Linux.

The other major component of the Linux operating system is the shell. The shell, often called the UNIX Shell. Is in interactive command-line interface.

A shell is launched in a tty.

TTY is a terminal in Linux.

The most commonly used shell in Linux is GNU Bash (Bourne again shell).
